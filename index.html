Quick Rules

  1. 4+ players, confined space, walking only.
  2. Host: Host New Game → Spin to pick the Hunter.
  3. Prey: Join Game → Spin to get a random 0:00–2:00 interval.
  4. Tap Enable sound, then Start. Your roll is final.
  5. Final 10 seconds are red; phone beeps at 0 and repeats.
  6. Hunter blindfolds and counts to 30; then hunts.
  7. Prey out if touched, if they touch the Hunter, or if they run/speak.
